Sam's Favorite Movies

My top 3 Movies of all Time

Forest Gump

Forest Gump is a cinematic Masterpeice that has a great story, great characters, great storytelling, an amazing soundtrack, and wholesome life lessons that anyone can apply to their own lives.

Shawshank Redemption

This movie's story is among the best ever created. It has very compelling characters that take the audience into the humanity of an incarcerated individual. It is probably the best prison break movie of all time.

The Pursuit of Happynes

This movie is very simple yet powerful in the way that it communicates simple principles of love and devotion to one's family. In many respects it is both hearbreaking and completely uplifting.
